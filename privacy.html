Who we are

Whitstable Dog Care is a family-run dog day care and boarding business run by Jack and Kim Rojas Powell, based on Walmer Road, Whitstable, Kent. We are the data controller for the personal information described on this page — that means we decide how it's used and are responsible for keeping it safe.

We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO application number C1970812, submitted 30 June 2026 — our registration number will be added here once our certificate arrives).

This page covers two things: how this website works, and what happens to your information once you and your dog become clients. If anything's unclear, email jack@whitstabledogcare.co.uk and we'll happily explain.

This website, in short

There's no booking form or account system on this website, so we don't collect or store any personal information ourselves through it. The only outside service that ever sees anything about your visit is Google Analytics — and only if you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you click "Necessary only" (or don't make a choice), nothing is sent to Google.

When you get in touch

When you contact us by phone, email or WhatsApp, we'll see your name, phone number and/or email address, and whatever else you choose to tell us — for example your dog's name, breed, and care needs. We use this only to arrange and provide day care or boarding, and we keep it for as long as we need it for that purpose, generally for the length of our relationship with you and your dog.

We don't sell, rent or share your information with anyone else, other than where we're legally required to.

Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which pages are useful, so we can improve it. The Analytics script is only loaded if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. If you click "Necessary only", or close the banner without choosing, the script is never requested and no data is sent to Google.

If you do accept, Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_ga_<container-id> Persists session state for Analytics 2 years

Your choice is remembered in your browser's local storage, so you won't be asked again on this device unless you clear your browser data. You can change your mind at any time by clearing site data/cookies for whitstabledogcare.co.uk and reloading the page.

Other services this site uses

Some parts of this site load resources directly from other providers — for example Google Fonts (for page text) and our Google Business Profile, Instagram, Yell and Nextdoor pages, which we link to. Visiting those providers' servers, or clicking through to their sites, is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours.

This website is hosted by Netlify, who may keep standard technical server logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and performance, in line with their own privacy policy.

When you become a client

When you complete our admissions and consent form, we collect:

  • Your name, address, phone number and email address
  • Your dog's name, date of birth, breed, sex, microchip number and medical information
  • Your vet's name, address and contact details
  • The name, address and contact details of your emergency contacts ("buddies")
  • Vaccination and flea/worm treatment dates
  • Dietary requirements, medical needs and behavioural notes for your dog
  • Insurance status
  • Your consents, and the date they were given

We collect this because we need it to look after your dog safely, and to be able to contact you or your vet in an emergency. Under UK data protection law, our lawful basis for this is contract performance. We don't use this information for marketing, and we don't sell it to anyone.

We only share it when it's necessary — with your vet or our nominated vet (Whitstable Bay Veterinary Centre) if your dog needs emergency treatment, or with your named emergency contacts if we can't reach you. We don't share it with anyone else.

We keep this information for as long as your dog is a client with us, and for two years after your last booking, so we can handle any insurance or liability queries that come up afterwards. After that, we delete it securely.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you — just ask and we'll send it to you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (we may need to retain some records for legal reasons)
  • Restrict how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Object to our use of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email jack@whitstabledogcare.co.uk or call 07840 469 646. We'll respond within one month.
