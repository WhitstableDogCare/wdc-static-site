Our process

How We Work

  1. 1

    Free Meet & Greet

    Come and visit with your dog — no commitment, no cost. We make sure it's a good fit for everyone.

  2. 2

    Paid Trial Day(s)

    Your dog spends time with us at their own pace, building confidence before regular bookings begin.

  3. 3

    Regular Day Care & Boarding

    Simple, clear pricing. Regular slots available for day care, boarding, or both.

Step 1

Free Meet & Greet

Before anything else, please take a moment to read our Policy & Procedures.

We'll begin with a free Meet & Greet in our indoor/outdoor dog area. We use dog gates and leads if needed, giving the dogs plenty of space to suss each other out. While they settle in, we'll chat through your requirements and answer any questions you have.

It's a relaxed, no-obligation visit — a chance for us to get to know each other and talk through how we can best support your dog's comfort, wellbeing and happiness.

Book a Free Meet & Greet

Step 2

Paid Trial(s)

What happens during a trial

After a successful Meet & Greet, your dog spends time in our care without you. We start with a short, pre-agreed session and gradually build up to a typical full day — always at a pace your dog is comfortable with.

Booking and payment

We'll email you our booking and consent form. Once that's returned, we agree a date and collect payment for the session.

Tailored to your dog

Trial periods aren't fixed in length — it depends entirely on how your dog settles in with their new surroundings and friends. This flexible approach means we get it right for every dog in our care.

Please note we are not set up to take last-minute bookings for dogs we do not know.

Step 3

Regular Day Care & Boarding

Making a booking

Once your dog has completed their trial, booking is straightforward. Get in touch by email, WhatsApp or phone and we'll confirm your session by email. Day care bookings can be made at short notice — for boarding and longer stays, please book as far ahead as possible to avoid disappointment.

Payment

Payment is due before your dog's stay begins, not at the time of booking. We'll confirm the amount and payment details when we confirm your session. See our Services & Pricing page for full rates.

Cancellations

We understand plans change, so we try to be fair:

  • 48+ hours' notice — no charge
  • Less than 48 hours' notice — full fee applies
  • No-show — full fee charged
